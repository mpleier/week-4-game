Crystal Collector!

You are given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific number to your total number.


You win the game by matching your total number to a random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.


You can also press number keys 1 - 4 on your key board to select the corresponding crystal.
